Aaron Kennedy

Aaron Kennedy likes to push himself to take on difficult challenges. He has studied computers science, political science and studio arts at the University of Minnesota. He has worked for National Instruments, volunteered in Peace Corps, taught high school in Kwethluk, Alaska, and taught middle school near Minneapolis.

Aaron is currently looking to get back to working with computers and technology as a web developer. He has returned to the University of Minnesota for a web developers bootcamp. It will be a challenge to learn all the technologies needed to implement web sites and applications, but he is looking forward to it.
